Clip not starting?

Stale cache or cookies can prevent the clipper from running. A quick browser reset almost always fixes it:

  1. Clear your browsing data — open your browser settings and clear cached files, cookies, and site data for this page.
  2. Hard-refresh the page — press Ctrl + Shift + R (Windows/Linux) or ⌘ + Shift + R (Mac) to force a clean reload.
  3. Try again — re-paste your stream URL and hit Capture Clip.

Still stuck? Try a different browser or disable any extensions (ad blockers, VPNs) for this site.
